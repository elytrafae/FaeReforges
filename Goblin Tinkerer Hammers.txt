## Hardmode ##

Cobalt Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 120% Reforge Cost
Palladium Hammer: 50% Negative Reforge Chance, 115% Reforge Cost

Mythril Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 115% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1 Defense
Orichalcum Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 115% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1% Movement Speed

Adamantite Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 110% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1% Damage
Titanium Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 110% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1% Crit Chance

Hallowed Hammer: 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 105% Reforge Cost, Weapon Effect: Dodging while holding the weapon gives you 10% Movement Speed and 10% Crit Chance for 10 seconds.
Frost Hammer (I know, this is no longer crafted with Hallowed Bars, blah blah blah): 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 105% Reforge Cost, Weapon Effect: +10% Damage against enemies under Frostbite
Forbidden Hammer (Read above): 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 105% Reforge Cost
	Weapon Effect: Non-summon weapons consume 10 Mana per hit, but deal 20 more damage. Weapons cannot be used while under 10 Mana, and deal 50% less damage while under Mana Sickness (except Magic weapons). Summons attempt to consume 40 Max Mana per slot they occupy for 20% increased damage.
	Old Weapon Effect: Non-summon weapons cost 10 more Mana, but deal 15 more damage. Summons attempt to consume 40 Max Mana per slot they occupy for 15% increased damage.

Chlorophyte Hammer: 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 95% Reforge Cost, Dealing damage with this weapon gives you +1 HP per second regeneration for 2 seconds.
Venomite Hammer (yes, new material. Cry about it): 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 95% Reforge Cost, Dealing damage with this weapon applies 3 seconds of Venom, but while holding this weapon, or having a summon active, makes you lose 2 Health per second.
Spectre Hammer: 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 95% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1.5% Chance to Dodge.
Shroomite Hammer: 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 95% Reforge Cost, Accessory: +2.5% Flight Time

Solar Hammer: 
	Weapon: Gain a ram dash while this weapon is held
	Accessory: +1.5% Damage Reduction. This effect is disabled for 5 seconds after taking more than 5 damage
Vortex Hammer: 
	Weapon: Deal a guaranteed crit every 10 seconds
	Accessory: +2% Damage and -1% Movement Speed
Nebula Hammer: Striking an enemy while holding an item reforged by this has a chance to drop a Heart.
	Weapon: Dealing damage drops light orbs that give you a stacking 15% Movement Speed and Flight Time buff. Stacks up to 3 times and lasts for 8 seconds.
	Accessory: +3% Crit Chance and +1% Damage Taken
Stardust Hammer: Items reforged with this summon a Stardust Eye while held, dealing the weapons's base damage as damage at a 10x slower rate than the weapon itself. It also deals the damage type of the held weapon. If the weapon is a summon wand, summons created by this item deal a guaranteed crit every 10 seconds.
	Weapon: Increase the weapon's damage by 140%, but halve its attack speed. Projectiles spawned by this weapon have their Iframes inflicted doubled.
	Accessory: When you die, you become your own minion for 2 seconds, immune to damage and can attack freely, after which you actually die. Your respawn time is reduced by the amount of time you were a minion. Additional accessories add to the duration. You cannot be targeted by enemies. If all players are minions, they all die instantly.
