Cobalt Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 120% Reforge Cost

Titanium Hammer: 45% Negative Reforge Chance, 110% Reforge Cost, Accessories reforged with this give +1% Crit Chance

Hallowed Hammer: 40% Negative Reforge Chance, 105% Reforge Cost, Dodging an attack while holding a weapon reforged by this gives you 10% Speed and 10% Crit Chance for 5 seconds.

Solar Hammer: You take 7% less damage while a weapon reforged with this is held. This effect is disabled for 5 seconds after taking damage.
